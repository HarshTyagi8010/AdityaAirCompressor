Maintenance Certificate

Official maintenance guidelines and service protocols

Daily Maintenance:

  • Clean the compressors daily
  • Drain out water from the air receiver tank daily

Weekly Maintenance:

  • Clean the air filter element once a week

Monthly Maintenance:

  • Change the oil every month
  • Change the air filter element after every 1 month (under normal conditions)

About Aditya Air Compressors

Aditya Air Compressors is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality air compressors in India. With years of experience in the industry, we provide reliable, efficient, and durable air compression solutions for various industrial applications.

Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry. We specialize in reciprocating air compressors ranging from 5HP to 15HP, suitable for small to large industrial operations.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and cost-effective compressed air solutions that power industries and businesses across India.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted partner for all compressed air needs, driving innovation and sustainability in industrial solutions.

Quality Assurance

Every product undergoes rigorous quality testing to ensure optimal performance and longevity. Our warranty and service support demonstrate our confidence in our products.
